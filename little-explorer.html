The story behind Little Explorer

Little Explorer was born from late-night coding sessions and real family frustrations.

As a dad and Head of Product at Livesport, I've seen how technology can either simplify or complicate our lives. After countless family trips where navigation apps confused more than helped, I decided to build something different.

Every feature in Little Explorer comes from a real family moment: the oversized buttons because my 5-year-old wanted to "help navigate," the offline maps because we lost signal during a camping trip, and the kid-friendly destinations because finding a good playground in a new city shouldn't be a treasure hunt.

Built during sleepless nights while my family slept, Little Explorer is my love letter to parents everywhere who believe that family time should be about connection, not wrestling with complicated apps.
